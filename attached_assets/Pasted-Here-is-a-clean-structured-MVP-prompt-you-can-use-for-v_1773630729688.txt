# MVP Prompt — OPDMS (Out-Patient Document Management System)

## Project Overview

The **Out-patient Document Management System (OPDMS)** is a modern **web-based healthcare portal** designed to digitize and streamline hospital outpatient services. The system addresses the inefficiencies of traditional paper-based medical records by providing a secure digital platform for **managing patient information, doctor schedules, laboratory results, treatment plans, and appointment scheduling**.

The platform aims to improve **healthcare accessibility, accuracy, and operational efficiency**, especially in response to challenges exposed during the COVID-19 pandemic, such as overcrowded waiting areas, manual appointment systems, and difficulty retrieving medical records. Through a centralized digital portal, patients and healthcare providers can interact, manage records, and schedule services efficiently.

The OPDMS will support **secure access to Electronic Health Records (EHR), automated notifications, appointment scheduling, laboratory results management, and real-time doctor availability updates**. It promotes a **patient-centered healthcare experience** while enabling medical practitioners to manage outpatient workflows more effectively.

The system will follow **modern web design standards** using a clean and accessible interface with a **light blue, black, and white color theme** to represent professionalism, trust, and healthcare reliability.
